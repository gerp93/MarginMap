Our Mission:

Welcome to Coyote Coders! Have you ever wanted to design your own game? Do you think it would be neat to be able to build your very own website from scratch? As long as you are curious and have access to a computer, you can! We at Coyote Coders believe that coding is fun and useful at any age, and have a mission to introduce elementary and middle school aged children to the wonders of coding.

What Is Coding?

Have you ever made up a secret code with your friends, that only you understand? Computer coding is similar because there are many different “languages” of code. If you know them, you can give instructions to your computer than only you, the computer, and others who know the language can understand.

Why Is Coding Important?

In addition to being fun, coding is a tool that runs our technology-based world. It’s in every app, program, and service you use. The more you understand computer coding, the more you understand how to rule the world! Just kidding, you won’t rule the world, but you will be able to contribute to improving it via the technology we use.
