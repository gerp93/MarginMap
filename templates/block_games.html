Getting Started

Welcome to Baboon Blocks. Blocks is a graphical version of Python that is easy to learn! Before you get started with the games, let’s learn all of the features so that you’ll be able to understand the game instructions:


This part of the page that you will use is called the programming environment.




The left half of the programming environment is where you can move code blocks. These are the instructions for the program to follow.




Once you’ve written the code, click the run button to see if it works.




Then, in the right window you can see the result of your code!




Change Myrtle's Color


In this game, you can tell our favorite turtle named Myrtle to change her color. Click on the colored box to choose a new color.


Myrtle Changes Shape


Change Myrtle's shape by clicking on the word "turtle" in the shape block. Then, you can select from the shapes available.




Moving Myrtle


Here you can tell Myrtle to moves around in the window. Instructions: • Myrtle can walk in two directions: forward and backward. You can change how far by changing the number in the move block. • Myrtle can take as many steps as you want her to, but be careful! If you tell her to take more than 200 steps she will walk right off the screen! • Myrtle can turn in two directions: left or right • You can tell Myrtle to turn around as many degrees as you want, but she might get dizzy!
